# Security Information for Omniverse Geckos

## Scope
This security policy covers:
- Main website (omniversodigital.github.io/omniverse-geckos)
- Smart contracts (when deployed)
- Associated infrastructure and services

## Vulnerability Disclosure
We welcome security researchers to report vulnerabilities responsibly:
1. Email security@omniversegeckos.com with details
2. Allow reasonable time for response and fix
3. Do not publicly disclose until resolved
4. Provide clear steps to reproduce

## Reward Program
We offer recognition and potential rewards for valid security findings:
- Critical vulnerabilities: Up to $1,000 USD in tokens
- High severity: Up to $500 USD in tokens  
- Medium severity: Up to $200 USD in tokens
- Low severity: Public recognition

## Out of Scope
The following are not eligible for rewards:
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security issues
- Denial of service attacks
- Spam or harassment
- Issues in third-party services we don't control

## Legal Safe Harbor
We will not pursue legal action against security researchers who:
- Act in good faith
- Follow responsible disclosure
- Do not harm users or steal data
- Report findings promptly

Thank you for helping keep Omniverse Geckos secure!
